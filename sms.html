CountAble SMS Program

CountAble sends text messages only to users (and accountability buddies) who explicitly opt in. This page describes the program.

What messages we send

  • One-time phone verification codes
  • Workout reminders you schedule yourself in the app
  • Weekly progress summaries and team recaps
  • Accountability-buddy invitations and updates (sent only after the recipient consents)

How opt-in works

Inside the CountAble app, a user enters their phone number, receives a one-time verification code by text, enters the code to prove they own the number, and then explicitly accepts an SMS consent notice describing message types, frequency, and opt-out instructions. No one receives recurring messages without completing these steps.

Message frequency

Frequency varies with your settings — typically up to a few messages per day (your own scheduled reminders) plus weekly summaries. Message and data rates may apply.

Opting out and getting help

  • Reply STOP at any time to stop all messages immediately.
  • Reply START to resume messages after stopping.
  • Reply HELP for help, or contact reportinator3000@gmail.com.
  • You can also pause SMS anytime from the app's settings.

Privacy

Phone numbers and SMS consent records are used solely to operate these features. We do not sell personal data, and no mobile information is shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See our Privacy Policy.
