Privacy Policy

CountAble (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

We may collect the following types of information:

User-Generated Data

Device & Usage Information

  • App usage data

Communication Data (if enabled)

We use your information to:

See our SMS Program page for full details, including opt-in, opt-out (STOP), and help (HELP) instructions.

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information with:

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations.

Upon account deletion:

However, no system is completely secure.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
